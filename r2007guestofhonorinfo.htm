Reunion By Lady Liberty 2007 Guests of Honor

(Note:   This page is a work in progress and more info and picture will be added soon.) 

The ACSAA Board of Directors and the Reunion 2007 Organizing Committee are  pleased to announce that the Reunion 2007 Guests of Honor will be "Sra" Herminia Granitto, "Profe" Donato Luis Laurita, Iris Lestani, and Raymond McKay. "Sra" Granitto and "Profe" Laurita are legacies at Lincoln School, having each devoted almost 50 years of service to Ward/AGHS and Lincoln Schools as Physical Education instructors. Given they are both approaching their mid 80’s, this may be one of the last opportunities for the ACSAA to invite them to a reunion. Iris Lestani dedicated over 39 years of her career to Lincoln School as an administrative secretary and for the last 25 years has been our ACSAA liaison person with Lincoln School. The annual ACSAA Scholarship is named after Iris.  Raymond McKay is our newest Guest of Honor. Ray joined the Lincoln School faculty in 1979 and has held positions of teacher, Guidance Office Director, Dean of Lincoln University College since 1992, and in 1998 added the title of Director of National Programs and Services. He was the Sponsor for the Class of 1981.
