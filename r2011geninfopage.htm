REUNION 2011 SUMMARY

Reunion 2011 was a tremendous success and attendance was beyond our most optimistic projections.   The Organizing Committee's original estimate was in the range of 200+ alumni including family members.  Actual attendance was close to 700 alumni and family at the three events including the anniversary reception,  banquet and asado.    A number of late requests to attend the reunion had to be turned away because of space limitations.  

We are grateful to all who attended and helped in the planning,  with particular mention of the individuals below  for their time and dedication to make Reunion 2011 and the Lincoln 75th Anniversary celebration a most memorable event:
